Bowen Chess Club is an officially registered club with the English Chess Federation (ECF), dedicated to promoting chess in the Greater Manchester area. We have an experienced team of ECF certified coaches providing systematic and professional training for chess players of all ages from 6 years old to adults. The club regularly organizes internal competitions and friendly matches, and recommends outstanding students to participate in ECF rated events and national championships. Chess is not only an intellectual sport, but also cultivates children's concentration, logical thinking, decision-making abilities and stress management skills, which greatly benefit their academic and future development.

Training Programmes

Beginner

Chess Beginners (Ages 6-9)

Chess Beginners (Ages 6-9)

Zero foundation introductory course, making children fall in love with chess through fun games and interactive teaching. Learn piece movements, basic rules, simple tactics and etiquette, cultivate concentration and logical thinking. ECF membership available upon course completion.

  • Piece movement rules and capturing methods
  • Basic opening principles (center control, piece development)
  • Simple tactics (checks, forks, pins)
  • 1.5 hours weekly × 10 weeks | £120/term

Intermediate

Chess Intermediate (Ages 10-14)

Chess Intermediate (Ages 10-14)

Enhance tactical skills and opening theory, learn endgame techniques and competition strategies. Participate in internal rated matches and friendly games, with opportunities to represent the club in ECF rated events.

  • Advanced tactics (discovered attacks, pins, deflection, clearance)
  • Common opening systems (Italian Opening, Spanish Opening)
  • Basic endgames (King and Pawn, Rook and Pawn)
  • 1.5 hours weekly × 10 weeks | £140/term
Learn More
Advanced Competition

Chess Advanced & Competition

Chess Advanced & Competition

Designed for students aspiring to competitive chess. In-depth study of opening theory, middlegame tactical combinations, and complex endgame techniques. Regular participation in ECF rated events, county championships and national youth tournaments, building personal opening repertoire and analyzing master games.

  • Personal opening repertoire construction and preparation
  • Middlegame tactical combinations and calculation training
  • ECF rated events and national tournament guidance
  • 2 hours weekly × 10 weeks | £160/term

Our Coaches

Our Coaches

All coaches hold ECF coaching qualifications with extensive teaching and competition experience. The head coach has won county championships, and several coaches hold National Master (NM) or Candidate Master (CM) titles.
