About Us

Leading Chinese Education in Manchester

Bridging cultures through quality education

Bowen Education Group is a registered educational institution in Manchester, UK, offering comprehensive Chinese language programmes from Foundation to A-Level, academic tutoring, chess club, badminton club, and government-funded community programmes.

We are committed to providing high-quality education that connects Eastern and Western cultures, helping students achieve their full potential through professional teaching and personalized support.
