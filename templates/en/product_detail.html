Frequently Asked Questions

What age groups are suitable?

Our courses are designed for different age groups, including:

  • Preschool: Ages 4-6
  • Primary: Ages 7-9
  • Intermediate: Ages 10-12
  • Advanced: Ages 13-15
  • GCSE/A-Level: Ages 15-18
  • Adults: 18+

Can I attend a free trial lesson?

Yes! We offer free trial lessons for new students. You can book a trial to experience our teaching environment and meet our teachers.

How do you assess my child's Chinese level?

We provide free Chinese language level assessment covering listening, speaking, reading and writing skills to help place your child in the most suitable class level.

What does the tuition fee include?

Tuition fees include:

  • Course textbooks and learning materials
  • Classroom teaching
  • Homework marking and feedback
  • Mid-term and end-of-term assessments
  • Certificate of completion
