About Parktastic

Bowen Education Group's Parktastic programme provides enriching outdoor learning experiences for children aged 5-11. We utilize the excellent park resources in the Manchester area to let children grow happily in nature through games, sports, arts, and team activities.

Every holiday, we organize different themed park activities, including drama performances, sports, art creation, and nature exploration. These activities not only let children enjoy outdoor time but also cultivate their social skills, creativity, and teamwork spirit.
