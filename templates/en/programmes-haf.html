What is HAF Program

Holiday Activities & Food Programme

Holiday Activities and Food (HAF) programme is a key community initiative funded by the UK government, aiming to provide eligible children with free healthy meals and engaging activities during school holidays.

Bowen Group actively participates in this programme, offering diverse holiday activities to local children, including Chinese cultural experiences, sports, arts & crafts, and educational games, allowing children to continue learning and growing during holidays.
