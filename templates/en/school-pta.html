About Our PTA

The Bowen Chinese School Parent-Teacher Association (PTA) is a voluntary organization formed by parents and teachers, working together to promote home-school cooperation and create a better learning environment for students.

Our Mission

  • Strengthen communication and cooperation between parents and school
  • Organize cultural activities and social events
  • Support school fundraising and improve teaching facilities
  • Provide advice on school education programmes

What We Do

Home-School Communication

We serve as a bridge between parents and teachers, facilitating regular communication through parent meetings, newsletters, and online platforms to ensure parents stay informed about their children's learning progress.

Cultural Events

We organize various cultural activities throughout the year, including Chinese New Year celebrations, Mid-Autumn Festival events, and educational workshops, helping children understand and appreciate Chinese culture.

Fundraising Activities

Through charity sales, sponsored events, and community activities, we raise funds to support school development, purchase teaching materials, and improve educational facilities.

Volunteer Services

Parent volunteers play an important role in school activities, from classroom assistance to event organization, contributing their time and expertise to support student development.

Join Us

All parents are welcome to join the PTA. Whether you can spare a few hours or want to be more actively involved, we welcome your participation. Together, we can create a better learning environment for our children.
