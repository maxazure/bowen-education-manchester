Common Questions

Quick answers to questions you may have

What age groups do you teach?

We offer courses for all ages, from Foundation (ages 4-6) to A-Level (ages 16-18), as well as adult learners. Each programme is tailored to the specific age group.

Do you offer trial lessons?

Yes! We offer free trial lessons for new students. This allows you to experience our teaching style and meet our teachers before enrolling.

What are your class sizes?

We maintain small class sizes with a maximum of 12 students per class to ensure personalized attention and effective learning.

How do I enroll my child?

Simply fill out the contact form above, call us, or email us to schedule an assessment. We'll help you find the right class for your child's level.
