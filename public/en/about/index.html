Our Story

From a small language school to a comprehensive education group

2018

Foundation

Bowen Chinese School was established in Manchester to provide quality Chinese education to the local community. Starting with just 30 students and 2 teachers, we began our journey in Chinese language education.

2019

Expansion

Launched Bowen Tuition Centre, specializing in GCSE and A-Level Chinese exam preparation. Established partnership with Henan University, China, for teacher training and cultural exchange programmes.

2020

Community Programmes

Introduced Chess Club and Badminton Club to promote holistic student development. Began organizing cultural activities and festival celebrations to strengthen community cohesion.

2021-2025

Growth & Recognition

Student enrollment grew to over 500, achieving a 100% GCSE pass rate. Became the recognized leading Chinese education provider in Greater Manchester, expanding into a comprehensive education group offering diverse programmes.

Our Core Values

The fundamental principles that guide everything we do

Excellence

We pursue the highest standards in teaching, learning, and student outcomes, committed to providing every student with the finest educational experience.

Care

Every student receives personalized attention and support. We care about each student's growth and holistic development, helping them reach their full potential.

Cultural Bridge

We connect Eastern and Western cultures, promoting understanding and appreciation of both Chinese and British heritage, cultivating students as global cultural ambassadors.

Community

We build strong partnerships with families, schools, and cultural organizations, creating a mutually supportive educational community for student growth.

Our Partnership

Henan University, China

We are proud to partner with Henan University, one of China's prestigious institutions with over 100 years of history. This collaboration enables:

  • Teacher training and professional development programmes
  • Cultural exchange opportunities for students
  • Access to authentic teaching materials and resources
  • Summer study programmes in China
  • HSK examination preparation support
