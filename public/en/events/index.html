Henan Partnership

UK-China Educational Exchange Programme

Henan University Partnership

Bowen Education Group has established a long-term strategic partnership with Henan University in China to jointly promote Sino-British educational and cultural exchanges.

Cooperation Content

  • Faculty Exchange: Henan University regularly sends excellent teachers to the UK for cultural exchange and teaching support
  • Student Exchange: Organize student exchange activities to promote friendship and understanding between young people from both countries
  • Cultural Activities: Jointly hold various Chinese culture promotion activities and academic seminars
  • Resource Sharing: Share teaching resources and research results

2025 Root-Seeking Journey

In the summer of 2025, Bowen Education organized outstanding student representatives to visit Henan, China, participating in the "Affection for China Root-Seeking Journey Meet Henan Root Camp" summer camp hosted by the China Overseas Chinese Federation.

Through ten days of cultural experience and on-site learning, campers deeply experienced cultural themes such as "martial spirit, family memories, intangible cultural heritage revival, center of heaven and earth":

  • Visited iconic cultural landmarks such as the Longmen Grottoes, Millennium City Park, and Shaolin Temple
  • Watched Shaolin Kung Fu performances and experienced Chinese martial arts
  • Entered Yu Opera classes to learn traditional opera art
  • Visited the Hakka Origin Memorial Hall in Luoyang to understand root culture
  • Experienced "Only Henan Drama Fantasy City" immersive performances
  • Visited Henan Science and Technology Museum, Henan Museum, and Kaifeng Museum

This root-seeking journey not only enriched students' cultural experience but also inspired their reflection on language learning and identity recognition.

Easter China Visit Program

Every year during the Easter holiday, we organize a two-week visit to China:

  • Date: Easter holiday each year
  • Target: Students aged 12-18
  • Itinerary: Henan (Kaifeng, Zhengzhou, Luoyang) + Beijing
  • Accommodation: University dormitories and selected hotels
  • Accompanied by: Professional tour leaders and Henan University volunteers

Root-Seeking Journey

Tracing Chinese Cultural Heritage, Inheriting Chinese Spirit

2025 Henan Root-Seeking Journey

In the summer of 2025, Bowen Education organized outstanding student representatives to visit Henan, China, participating in the "Affection for China Root-Seeking Journey Meet Henan Root Camp" summer camp hosted by the China Overseas Chinese Federation and organized by the Henan Provincial Overseas Chinese Federation and Kaifeng City Overseas Chinese Federation. This event brought together overseas Chinese teenagers from around the world, experiencing the profound heritage of Chinese civilization through ten days of cultural experience and on-site learning, seeking cultural roots and inheriting Chinese spirit.

Cultural Theme Experience

During this summer camp, campers explored the Central Plains on foot, deeply experiencing cultural themes such as "martial spirit and cultural heritage, family memories, intangible cultural heritage revival, center of heaven and earth, passing on the torch, and Song Dynasty elegance."

Visiting Cultural Landmarks

They visited iconic cultural landmarks such as the Longmen Grottoes, Millennium City Park, and Shaolin Temple, feeling the inheritance of thousand-year cultural heritage and martial arts spirit. The campers watched wonderful Shaolin Kung Fu performances and participated in basic movement practice, personally experiencing the "martial spirit" power of Chinese martial arts.

  • Longmen Grottoes - Experience the profundity of Buddhist art
  • Millennium City Park - Experience the prosperity of Song Dynasty culture
  • Shaolin Temple - Learn the essence of Chinese martial arts

Intangible Cultural Heritage Experience

In the intangible cultural heritage experience session, children entered Yu Opera classes, wearing costumes to learn singing techniques, experiencing the artistic beauty of "Song Dynasty elegance"; they also made traditional kites by hand, understanding the meanings behind the patterns, and appreciating the "revival" charm of intangible cultural heritage projects.

Traditional Crafts Learning

In the traditional crafts course at Henan University, campers assembled mortise and tenon structures from ancient Chinese architecture, understanding the craftsman spirit of "passing on the torch" through practice.

  • Yu Opera Class - Learn traditional opera art
  • Kite Making - Experience traditional folk crafts
  • Mortise and Tenon Structure - Understand ancient architectural wisdom

Tracing Cultural Roots

In the journey of tracing cultural roots, students visited the Hakka Origin Memorial Hall in Luoyang, gaining a deep understanding of the historical trajectory of Hakka ancestors migrating from the Central Plains to the south, reflecting on their own cultural identity and the formation of "family memories." Through rich exhibits and explanations, children personally felt the historical weight and emotional depth carried by "root culture."

Immersive Cultural Experience

Students also specially visited "Only Henan Drama Fantasy City," immersively listening to stories about "Yellow River, land, grain, and inheritance." In multiple theater performances and interactive scenes, they deeply appreciated the cultural foundation and spiritual context of Chinese agricultural civilization, further understanding the geographical significance and cultural value of "center of heaven and earth."

Expanding Horizons

In addition, campers also visited:

  • Henan Science and Technology Museum - Learn about modern technological development
  • Henan Museum - Appreciate Central Plains cultural relics
  • Kaifeng Museum - Explore the historical changes of the ancient capital

Gradually establishing a sense of closeness and belonging to Chinese civilization through diverse cultural experiences.

A Meaningful Journey of Growth

This root-seeking journey not only enriched students' cultural experience but also inspired their reflection on language learning and identity recognition. Everyone expressed that this was an extraordinarily meaningful growth journey that allowed them to more truly experience the power of "roots."

Student Testimonials

"This journey made me truly understand what 'roots' mean. Seeing the Buddha statues at Longmen Grottoes and hearing the morning and evening bells at Shaolin Temple, I felt the profundity of Chinese culture." Student Representative

"During the ten days in Henan, I not only learned a lot of historical knowledge but more importantly found a sense of cultural identity. I am proud of my cultural heritage." Student Representative

About Bowen Education

As the only Ofsted-registered Chinese and Chinese culture education institution in the UK, Bowen Education has always been committed to building a bridge to Chinese culture for overseas Chinese teenagers. Being invited to participate in the national-level "Affection for China" exchange program this time is a high recognition of our long-term efforts and teaching achievements.

In the future, Bowen Education will continue to provide more high-quality cultural exchange opportunities, allowing Chinese culture to be passed on overseas, endless and eternal.
