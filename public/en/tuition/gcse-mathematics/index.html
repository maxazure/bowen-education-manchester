GCSE Mathematics Tutoring

Expert GCSE Maths tutoring with focus on problem-solving and exam technique

Course Description

Course Overview

Our GCSE Mathematics Tutoring provides comprehensive, personalized support for students preparing for their GCSE Mathematics examinations across all major exam boards including AQA, Edexcel, and OCR. Designed for students in Years 10-11 (ages 14-16), our programme covers all topics from Foundation to Higher tier, focusing on building strong conceptual understanding, problem-solving skills, and exam confidence. With experienced teachers and proven teaching methods, we help students achieve their target grades and beyond.

GCSE14-16

Learning Objectives

  • Master all GCSE Mathematics topics with deep conceptual understanding GCSE
  • Develop advanced problem-solving and analytical reasoning skills
  • Build confidence in tackling challenging exam questions
  • Perfect examination techniques and time management strategies
  • Achieve target grades from 4-9 on both Foundation and Higher tiers 4-9

Course Coverage

Number :

  • Types of numbers, operations, fractions, decimals, percentages
  • Ratio, proportion, standard form, surds, indices
  • Number sequences, problem-solving with numbers

Algebra :

  • Algebraic expressions, equations, inequalities
  • Graphs of linear, quadratic, cubic functions
  • Simultaneous equations, factorization, completing the square
  • Sequences, iteration, proof

Geometry & Measures :

  • Properties of shapes, angles, constructions
  • Pythagoras theorem, trigonometry (SOHCAHTOA)
  • Circles, vectors, transformations
  • Area, volume, surface area calculations

Statistics & Probability :

  • Data collection, representation (charts, graphs, tables)
  • Averages, measures of spread, cumulative frequency
  • Probability theory, tree diagrams, Venn diagrams
  • Statistical analysis and interpretation

Teaching Features

Personalized Learning Plans : Initial assessment identifies strengths and gaps, allowing creation of tailored learning plans targeting individual needs.

Small Group or 1-to-1 : Choose between small group sessions (maximum 6 students) for collaborative learning or one-to-one tutoring for intensive personalized support.

Exam Board Specific : Teaching aligned to specific exam board requirements (AQA, Edexcel, OCR) with board-specific question practice.

Regular Assessments : Topic tests and full mock examinations track progress and build exam familiarity and confidence.

Digital Resources : Access to online learning platform with video tutorials, interactive exercises, and additional practice materials.

Teaching Methodology

Conceptual Understanding : We prioritize deep understanding over memorization, ensuring students grasp the "why" behind mathematical procedures.

Worked Examples : Detailed step-by-step solutions to exam questions demonstrate effective problem-solving approaches.

Guided Practice : Scaffolded practice with decreasing support builds independence and confidence.

Exam Technique : Specific strategies for different question types, mark allocation awareness, and effective time management.

Mistake Analysis : Learning from common errors and misconceptions prevents repeated mistakes in examinations.

Course Structure

Foundation Tier (Grades 1-5):

  • Core mathematical concepts and techniques
  • Building numerical fluency and algebraic skills
  • Practical problem-solving in real contexts
  • Focus on achieving secure Grade 4 or higher

Higher Tier (Grades 4-9):

  • Advanced topics including surds, vectors, and circle theorems
  • Complex problem-solving requiring multiple steps
  • Algebraic proof and abstract reasoning
  • Targeting Grades 7-9 for top university pathways

Suitable For

This tutoring programme is ideal for:

  • Students in Years 10-11 preparing for GCSE Mathematics examinations GCSE10-11
  • Those struggling with specific topics needing targeted support
  • High-achievers aiming for Grades 8-9 seeking challenge and extension 8-9
  • Students resitting GCSE Mathematics to improve their grade GCSE
  • Year 9 students preparing early for GCSE content GCSE9

Qualified Teachers

Our mathematics tutors are qualified teachers or graduates with mathematics degrees and extensive GCSE teaching experience. Many are current or former GCSE examiners with insider knowledge of marking schemes and assessment criteria. All tutors have enhanced DBS checks and proven track records of helping students achieve significant grade improvements.
