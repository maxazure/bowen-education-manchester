2. Services

Bowen Education Group provides educational services including but not limited to:

  • Chinese language education
  • Academic tutoring (GCSE and A-Level)
  • Chess club training
  • Badminton club training
  • Holiday activities programmes (HAF)
  • Cultural events and activities

3. Registration and Enrollment

3.1 Eligibility

Services are generally available to children aged 5-16 years. Some programmes may have specific age requirements.

3.2 Enrollment Process

  • Complete registration forms with accurate information
  • Provide necessary documentation

3.3 Parental Consent

For students under 18, registration requires parental or guardian consent.

4.1 Payment Terms

  • Payment plans may be available upon request

4.2 Refund Policy

  • No refunds after the second week of term

4.3 Late Payment

Failure to pay fees by the due date may result in suspension of services.

All students and parents are expected to:
