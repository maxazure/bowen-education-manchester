Curriculum

Chinese Curriculum

Chinese Course Introduction

Bowen Chinese School's curriculum system covers all stages of learning from Pinyin Foundation to Advanced Reading and Writing, with clear grade divisions and progressive content.

The school adopts a systematic Huawen textbook series, combined with Topic-based Learning, allowing students to learn language and understand culture in authentic contexts. Classes not only focus on phonetics, vocabulary, and grammar training, but also integrate Chinese history, traditional festivals, cultural stories, and modern life elements, helping children comprehensively improve in "listening, speaking, reading, and writing."

Each week, students will participate in:
- Two hours of Huawen course learning
- One hour of language application class
- Half an hour of one-on-one online tutoring

Huawen Textbook Learning

The Huawen main course focuses on developing language fundamentals and reading and writing abilities, helping students systematically master Chinese knowledge.

The language application class uses interactive teaching and situational expression to enable students to "express in Chinese and think in Chinese" in real environments.

This "main course + application course" teaching model ensures both the systematization of language learning and enhanced practical application abilities, allowing students to naturally use Chinese both inside and outside the classroom.

Additionally, the school organizes students to participate in YCT (Youth Chinese Test) and HSK (Chinese Proficiency Test) every academic year, testing learning outcomes with international standards and allowing students to make steady progress along a clear learning path.

Bowen Chinese Language Application Class (Compulsory)

The core of language learning lies in application. Bowen's Chinese language application class aims to make Chinese "come alive and be used" for children through rich and diverse classroom formats, truly transforming language into a tool for expression and thinking.

Course Content Includes:

  • Picture Book Reading Class: Cultivate language sense and reading comprehension through stories
  • Recitation Class: Train pronunciation, intonation, and language expressiveness
  • Speech and Debate Class: Develop confident expression and logical thinking
  • Cultural Extension Class: Learn about traditional Chinese festivals and folk culture
  • Pinyin Intensive Class: Build a solid foundation in phonetics and improve pronunciation accuracy
  • Radical Intensive Class: Help students understand Chinese character structure and increase character recognition
  • Real-life Experience Class: Simulate real-life situations and use Chinese in practice

Immersive Learning Experience

To achieve a truly immersive learning experience, the school arranges:
- Three hours of Chinese immersive classroom learning per week, with full Chinese communication in class
- Half an hour of free one-on-one online tutoring every Saturday, with teachers providing targeted improvement based on individual student needs

Through the "classroom + application + personalized tutoring" learning system, Bowen Chinese School helps students maximize Chinese practice, making language learning part of their daily lives.

1-on-1 Chinese Online Class (Free for Bowen Students)

Bowen Chinese School has deep cooperation with Chinese as a Foreign Language research teams from domestic universities, providing long-term free 1-on-1 customized Chinese online tutoring for enrolled students.

Course content covers pinyin, conversation, writing, and cultural extension, designed by professional teachers based on students' age, proficiency level, and learning goals. Students can flexibly learn online, consolidate classroom knowledge, strengthen language application, and achieve synchronized online and offline growth.

This unique teaching support system ensures every Bowen student receives continuous attention and personalized guidance, truly achieving "learning with gain, learning with success."

Homework Class (Chinese Learning Support)

To help students consolidate classroom knowledge and improve learning efficiency, Bowen Chinese School offers a "Homework Support Class" every Sunday afternoon.

This course is open to all enrolled students, with Bowen education interns and teaching assistant teams providing one-on-one or small group tutoring, focusing on helping students complete their weekly Chinese homework and reinforce classroom content.

Tutoring Content Includes:

  • Text review and vocabulary consolidation
  • Grammar and sentence pattern explanation
  • Listening and speaking practice and oral expression
  • Reading and writing skill improvement guidance
  • Homework checking and personalized feedback

The homework class not only allows students to promptly digest knowledge and fill in gaps after class, but also greatly reduces parents' pressure in home Chinese tutoring.

Through collaboration between teachers and intern assistants, students can consolidate knowledge in a relaxed learning atmosphere and develop good habits of independent learning and standard writing.

Bowen Chinese School believes that continuous learning support is an important guarantee for language growth. The establishment of the homework class provides students with systematic, warm, and efficient learning companionship outside the classroom, making Chinese truly part of their lives.

Parent-Teacher Association (PTA)

Home and School Together, Nurturing the Future

Bowen Chinese School believes that education is never a one-way street from the school alone, but the result of joint efforts between families and schools.

To strengthen home-school communication and promote students' comprehensive development, the school has established the Parent-Teacher Association (PTA), building a bridge of openness, cooperation, and mutual benefit between parents and teachers.

PTA Mission

Bowen PTA is committed to:

  • Promoting Home-School Communication Keeping parents informed about their children's learning and school development
  • Jointly Advancing Educational Quality Making teaching more responsive to student needs through feedback and suggestions
  • Organizing School Activities and Celebrations Assisting in planning cultural festival activities such as Chinese New Year, Mid-Autumn Festival, and Dragon Boat Festival
  • Building a Parent Exchange Platform Sharing educational insights and learning experiences, forming a warm and supportive parent community
  • Supporting School Public Welfare and Community Projects Participating in volunteer services, cultural promotion, and community cooperation

PTA Structure

The PTA consists of parent representatives, teacher representatives, and school administrative members.

  • Parent Representatives: Parents who voluntarily join to voice the concerns of classroom parent groups
  • Teacher Representatives: Recommended by the school to participate in discussions on teaching and student development
  • School Representatives: Principals and academic directors serve as advisors, responsible for coordinating and supporting PTA work

PTA Activities and Projects

Throughout the year, the PTA organizes and participates in various activities, including:

  • Festival Celebrations and Cultural Activities: Assisting with venue setup, performance preparation, and student participation organization
  • Charity and Community Service: Organizing charity sales, community lectures, and cultural exchange activities
  • Parent Workshops: Inviting experts to explain parent-child communication, learning methods, and psychological development
  • Campus Volunteer Projects: Assisting with library management, event photography, and safety supervision

These activities not only enrich school life but also allow parents to better understand their children's growth environment through participation, strengthening the cohesion of the school community.

Joining PTA

We welcome more parents to join the PTA and work together to create a higher quality, warmer learning environment for our children.

Parents can sign up as volunteer members through their class teachers or contact the PTA coordinator directly for more information.

Contact Information:
- Email: pta@boweneducation.org
- Meeting Frequency: One regular meeting per term + preparatory meetings before major events

Registration & Trial

Registration and Trial Classes

Welcome to Bowen Chinese School!

Bowen Chinese School welcomes all students who are eager to learn Chinese and understand Chinese culture.

We offer free trial classes for all new students, helping parents and children better understand our curriculum, teaching styles, and classroom atmosphere to find the most suitable learning path.

Registration and Trial Process

Step 1: Fill Out the Registration Form

Parents can fill out the online registration form through the school website or by scanning the WeChat QR code, and select a suitable trial class date and time slot.

Information to provide includes student name, age, Chinese proficiency level, contact details, and interested course categories.

Step 2: Course Matching and Trial Arrangement

The school will arrange an appropriate trial class based on the student's age and language proficiency.

Staff will confirm the trial class, time, and classroom information via email or text 12 days before the trial.

Step 3: On-site Trial Experience

Students attend class at the scheduled time. The trial class will be taught by official teachers.

During class, teachers will observe the student's language foundation and learning interests to provide reference for subsequent class placement.

Step 4: Trial Feedback and Class Placement Recommendations

After the trial, teachers will communicate with parents to provide feedback on the student's classroom performance and suitable class level.

Parents can choose to enroll in the appropriate course based on the feedback.

Step 5: Confirm Enrollment and Payment

After determining the class, parents complete payment and registration, and the student officially joins Bowen Chinese School.

The Academic Affairs Office will send enrollment notifications and timetable information, and your child can officially begin their learning journey.
